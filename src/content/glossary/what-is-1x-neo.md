## What is 1X Neo?

The 1X Neo is a type of computer chip designed by Huawei, a Chinese technology company. This chip is special because it's made to handle artificial intelligence (AI) tasks, like understanding speech or recognizing images. At a high level, the 1X Neo chip works by processing lots of information quickly, so your device can perform complex tasks like translating languages or suggesting what you might like to watch next. Think of it like a super-smart, tiny brain that helps your device make decisions and learn from the data it collects. The 1X Neo chip is part of a larger category of chips called neural processing units (NPUs), which are designed specifically for AI tasks. These chips are important because they help devices like smartphones and tablets perform complex tasks without needing to send data to a central server, which can be slow and insecure.
