## What is AI Impact Assessment?
AI Impact Assessment is the process of evaluating how artificial intelligence (AI) systems might affect people, businesses, and society as a whole. It's like a thorough check-up to see if an AI system is safe and fair. This assessment considers various factors, including the potential benefits and risks of using an AI system, its impact on jobs, and how it might change the way people live and work. For instance, an AI system designed to help doctors diagnose diseases might be assessed for its potential to improve patient outcomes, but also for its potential to replace human doctors. The goal of AI Impact Assessment is to identify potential problems before they happen and to find ways to mitigate them.

## Think of It Like This
Imagine you're introducing a new pet to your household. You'd want to think about how it might affect your family, your home, and even your neighbors. Will it get along with your other pets? Will it make too much noise? Will it need special care? AI Impact Assessment is similar. It's a way to think carefully about the potential effects of introducing an AI system into our lives. Just as you wouldn't want a pet to cause problems, we don't want AI systems to cause harm or make our lives worse. By thinking ahead, we can try to prevent problems and make sure AI systems are beneficial for everyone.

## Why Should You Care?
AI Impact Assessment matters because AI systems are becoming more common in our daily lives. They're used in things like social media, online shopping, and even self-driving cars. These systems can be very powerful and have a big impact on our lives. For example, an AI system that helps with hiring decisions might accidentally discriminate against certain groups of people. Or, an AI system that controls self-driving cars might make a mistake and cause an accident. By assessing the potential impact of these systems, we can help ensure that they're fair, safe, and beneficial for everyone.

## Where You've Already Seen It
You might have already seen AI Impact Assessment in action without realizing it. For instance, social media companies like Facebook and Twitter have been working to assess the impact of their AI systems on society. They're trying to figure out how to prevent the spread of misinformation and ensure that their platforms are fair and safe for everyone. Another example is the development of self-driving cars. Companies like Tesla and Waymo are assessing the potential impact of their AI systems on road safety and trying to mitigate any potential risks. Even streaming services like Netflix use AI Impact Assessment to ensure that their recommendation systems are fair and don't discriminate against certain types of content.

## The One Thing to Remember
The key thing to remember about AI Impact Assessment is that it's a way to think carefully about the potential effects of AI systems on our lives. By assessing these effects, we can try to prevent problems and ensure that AI systems are beneficial for everyone. It's an important part of making sure that AI is used in a responsible and safe way.
